<p>Mental health refers to the state of our emotional, psychological, and social well-being.</p>
<p>It impacts the way we feel, think and act, making it important in all areas of our lives.</p>
<p>Mental health plays an important role in the way we deal with stress, how we relate to others, and the decisions we make in our daily lives.</p>
<p>Without positive mental health, it will be almost impossible to realize your full potential, work productively, make a meaningful contribution to your community, or handle the stress that comes with life.</p>
<p>So what can you do to ensure you have a healthy mental state?</p>
<p>Well, there are various ways to maintain positive mental health and live a more fulfilling and enjoyable life.</p>
<p>Besides seeking professional help if you need it, you should make time to connect with others, think positively about yourself and get physically active.</p>
<p>Also, make sure you get enough rest,  you’re mindful of your present moment, and be helpful to others.</p>
<p>A healthy mental state will help you leverage your Everyday Power to achieve the success you seek and live a purposeful life.</p>
